Notice 797

(Rev. September 2025)

Possible Federal Tax Refund Due to the Earned Income Credit (EIC)

Department of the Treasury — Internal Revenue Service

You may be able to claim the EIC if: You worked but earned less than certain amounts, and you have qualifying children or meet other requirements.

Part I — Your Information

Part II — Eligibility Check

1. Did you have at least one qualifying child?
2. Number of qualifying children
3. Filing status
4. Earned income from work

Part III — EIC Estimate

5. Estimated EIC amount (based on income and children)
6. Did you already receive an EIC refund?
7. If yes, amount received
8. Additional refund owed (if any)

Part IV — How to Claim

To claim the EIC, file Form 1040, 1040-SR, or 1040-NR with Schedule EIC if you have qualifying children.

You must include a valid SSN for yourself and any qualifying child.

If you don't have a SSN, apply for one at socialsecurity.gov.
