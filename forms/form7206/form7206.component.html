Form 7206

(2024)

Self-Employed Health Insurance Deduction

Department of the Treasury — Internal Revenue Service

Use this form to figure your self-employed health insurance deduction. Attach to Form 1040, 1040-SR, or 1040-NR.

Part I — General Information

Part II — Self-Employment Test

Part III — Deduction Computation

1. Self-employed health insurance paid (from Schedule C, line 15 or Schedule F, line 16)
2. Add amounts from any Forms K-1 (Form 1065 or 1120-S)
3. Total self-employed health insurance (line 1 + line 2)
4. Enter net profit from self-employment (from Schedule C, line 31 or Schedule F, line 34)
5. Enter net farm profit (from Schedule F, line 34 or K-1)
6. Add lines 4 and 5
7. Enter amount of deduction (line 3 or line 6, whichever is less)

Part IV — Who Paid the Premiums

Did you pay premiums for:
• Yourself?
• Your spouse?
• Your dependents?
