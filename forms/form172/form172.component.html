Form 172

Department of the Treasury - Internal Revenue Service

Net Operating Losses (NOLs) for Individuals, Estates, and Trusts

Attach to Form 1040, 1041, or 990-T.

Taxpayer Information

Part I - Net Operating Loss Computation
1 Gross income (excluding NOL) (see instructions)
2 Deductions (see instructions)
3 Net operating loss
Part II - Deductions
1 Self-employment tax deduction
2 Itemized deductions or standard deduction
3 Section 199A deduction (QBI)
4 Other deductions
5 Total deductions
Part III - NOL Available This Year
1 NOL from prior years carried forward
2 NOL generated this year (from Part I, line 3)
3 Total NOL available
Part IV - NOL Deduction
1 Taxable income before NOL deduction
2 NOL deduction (see instructions - limited to 80%)
3 Remaining NOL to carry forward
