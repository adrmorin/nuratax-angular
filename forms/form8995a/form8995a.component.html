Form 8995-A

(2024)

Qualified Business Income Deduction for S-Corporations and Partnerships

Department of the Treasury — Internal Revenue Service

Use this form for S-Corporations and Partnerships to report the qualified business income (QBI) deduction allocated to shareholders or partners.

Part I — Entity Information

Part II — Partner/Shareholder Information

Part III — Entity QBI Calculation

1. Qualified business income (QBI) from trade or business
2. W-2 wages paid by the trade or business
3. Unadjusted basis of qualified property (UBIA)
4. Net capital gain
5. Taxable income (lesser of line 1 or entity's taxable income)

Part IV — Deduction Calculation for Entity

6. 20% of QBI
7. 50% of W-2 wages
8. 25% of W-2 wages + 2.5% of UBIA
9. Greater of line 7 or line 8
10. 20% of (taxable income - net capital gain)
11. QBI deduction (lesser of line 6, 9, or 10)

Part V — Allocation to Partner/Shareholder

12. Total QBI deduction (from line 11)
13. Partner/Shareholder allocation (line 12 × ownership %)
