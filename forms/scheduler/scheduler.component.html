Schedule R

(2024)

Credit for the Elderly or the Disabled

Department of the Treasury — Internal Revenue Service

Use this schedule to figure the credit for the elderly or the disabled. Attach to Form 1040 or 1040-SR.

Part I — Filing Status and Age

Part II — Age/Disability Test

1. Check the box that applies:
2. Enter date of birth
3. If married filing jointly, spouse's date of birth

Part III — Credit Computation

4. Enter the amount from Form 1040, line 11
5. Enter the amount from Schedule 3, line 3 (if any)
6. Adjusted gross income (line 4 minus line 5)
7. Base amount (see instructions): Single: $5,000; MFJ: $7,500; MFS: $3,750
8. Subtract line 7 from line 6 (if zero or less, stop here)
9. Credit percentage (based on line 8)
10. Credit (multiply line 8 by percentage on line 9)
