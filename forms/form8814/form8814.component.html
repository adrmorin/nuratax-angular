Form 8814

(2024)

Parents' Election To Report Child's Interest and Dividends

Department of the Treasury — Internal Revenue Service

Use this form if you are a parent electing to include your child's interest and dividends on your return.

Part I — Child Information

Part II — Child's Interest and Dividends

1a. Child's taxable interest
1b. Child's tax-exempt interest
2. Child's ordinary dividends
3. Qualified dividends (if any)
4. Total (lines 1a + 1b + 2)
5. Investment interest expense
6. Net investment income (line 4 minus line 5)

Part III — Election Computation

7. Base amount ($2,500 for 2024)
8. Subtract line 7 from line 4
9. Amount to include on parent's return
10. Additional tax (if any)

Part IV — Election

I understand that this election is irrevocable and that I must include the amounts from line 9 in my gross income.
