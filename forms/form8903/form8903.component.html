Form 8903

(2025)

Domestic Production Activities Deduction

Department of the Treasury — Internal Revenue Service

Use this form to figure your domestic production activities deduction (DPAD). Attach to Form 1040, 1041, or 1120.

Part I — Income and Deduction

1. Gross receipts from qualified production
2. Cost of goods sold allocable to qualified production
3. Qualified production gross income (line 1 minus line 2)
4. Total gross income from all sources
5. Allocable deductions
6. Taxable income before DPAD (line 4 minus line 5)

Part II — DPAD Calculation

7. Multiply line 6 by 9% (0.09)
8. Income attributable to qualified production (50% of line 3)
9. Tentative DPAD (lesser of line 7 or line 8)
10. Form 1040: Enter amount from Form 1040, line 11
11. Form 1120: Enter taxable income from Form 1120, line 11
12. Domestic production activities deduction
